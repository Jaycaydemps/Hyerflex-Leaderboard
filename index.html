Section 01

The three behaviors
we're rewarding

Every point an employee earns traces back to one of three target behaviors. The model is additive — doing more of any behavior always helps your rank.

Behavior 01

Shift pickups

The highest-frequency behavior. Every completed shift earns base points regardless of location.

10 pts per shift
Behavior 02

Cross-location work

Working outside your home store earns the standard shift points plus a location bonus — no separate mechanic needed.

10+8 pts per cross-loc shift
Behavior 03

Skill activation

The first time each approved skill is used in a month earns a bonus. Resets every month — rewarding breadth consistently over time.

+15 pts per skill, first use each month

Section 04

Anti-gaming
guardrails

The model is designed so that points can only be earned through genuine completed work — not applications, not repeats.

Completion required

Shift points only award on completed shifts. No-shows remove points from the monthly total.

One activation per skill

Skill bonus awards once per skill per month. Repeating the same skill after activation earns standard shift points only.

Cross-loc needs completion

The location bonus applies only to fully completed cross-location shifts, not applications or partial shifts.

Monthly reset

All scores and skill activations reset on the 1st. Previous month archives remain visible; all-time totals tracked separately.
